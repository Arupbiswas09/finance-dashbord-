# Monthly Newsletter Generation - Demo Prompt

## Quick Copy-Paste Prompt:

```
Create a comprehensive monthly financial newsletter for [MONTH] [YEAR]. 

CONTENT STRUCTURE:
1. **Header Section**: Welcome message with overview of the month's key financial highlights and market conditions.

2. **Market Overview**: Current economic trends, inflation rates, interest rate changes, and their impact on businesses. Include specific data points and percentages.

3. **Tax & Compliance Updates**: Latest tax regulations, filing deadlines, compliance requirements, and any changes to Belgian tax law. Include specific dates and action items.

4. **Key Financial Metrics**: Highlight important financial figures such as:
   - Pension contribution limits
   - VAT rates and deadlines
   - Tax brackets
   - Important financial thresholds

5. **Action Items & Deadlines**: List critical dates and deadlines for the upcoming month, including:
   - VAT filing dates
   - Tax payment deadlines
   - Compliance submission dates
   - Important financial planning milestones

6. **Business Insights**: Practical advice for business owners covering:
   - Cash flow management tips
   - Expense optimization strategies
   - Investment opportunities
   - Risk management recommendations

7. **Sector Analysis**: Industry-specific insights and trends affecting different business sectors in Belgium.

8. **Year-End Planning** (if applicable): Strategic advice for year-end financial planning, tax optimization, and preparation for the next fiscal year.

9. **Footer Section**: Contact information, company details, and unsubscribe information.

TONE & STYLE:
- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise language
- Use bullet points and numbered lists for readability
- Include specific numbers, dates, and percentages
- Action-oriented with clear next steps

CONTENT REQUIREMENTS:
- Write in [LANGUAGE]
- Target word count: 2000-2500 words
- Include 7-10 substantial sections
- Each section should be 3-5 paragraphs with detailed analysis
- Use credible sources and cite them appropriately
- Include specific Belgian financial data and regulations
- Make content actionable and practical for business owners

FOCUS AREAS:
- Belgian tax system and regulations
- EU financial directives affecting Belgium
- Local business trends and market conditions
- Practical financial advice for SMEs
- Compliance and regulatory updates
```

## Alternative Shorter Version:

```
Generate a monthly financial newsletter for [MONTH] [YEAR] covering:
- Economic trends and market conditions
- Tax updates and compliance deadlines
- Key financial metrics (pension limits, VAT rates, tax brackets)
- Action items with specific dates
- Business insights and practical advice
- Sector-specific analysis
- Year-end planning strategies (if applicable)

Write in a professional, clear style with specific data points, actionable recommendations, and credible sources. Target 2000-2500 words across 7-10 detailed sections.
```
